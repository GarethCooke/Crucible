## Notes on the demo 5 detour

Worth recording briefly because demo 6+ scoping should benefit from the lesson:

- The 2× p99.9 target in the original allocators brief was aspirational. The empirical reality was 1.23× at best on this workload. A pre-headline calibration step caught it before the post was written rather than after, which is the right place to catch it — but a calibration step should have been planned into the original brief from the start, not added after the user ran into trouble. Future brief template: include a "preflight calibration" task whenever the post's thesis depends on a specific magnitude.
- Methodology consistency (headless vs graphical) was almost lost to brief omission and only caught because the user remembered demos 1-4 were headless. The followup brief patched it before the headline capture went out. Future brief template: capture environment is an explicit field, defaulted to whatever the prior demos used.
- The `cset` v1 cpuset on the reference machine restricting PID 1's affinity was a separate environmental gotcha discovered during this demo's calibration. It's documented in the calibration-notes README's capture procedure (`sudo cset shield --reset` step). Consider whether `BRIEF.md` or `crucible-handover.md` should hoist this into the global precondition checklist for future captures.
